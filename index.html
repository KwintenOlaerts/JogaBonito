ABOUT

Milan
Musician, producer, DJ, percussionist and electronic music enthusiast, Milan Beanz originates from Ghent, Belgium. In 2004 he got inspired by James holden with his amazing remix of “The Sky Was Pink” by Nathan Fake and later on by the Berlin underground music scene. Never afraid to go too deep, his sounds are his own therapy. Having grown up on his father’s eclectic combination of Rock/Pop music (Pink Floyd, The Beatles,..), European electronica (Underworld), synth-infused 80's music (A Split Second)… all which became infused with his own love for hip-hop and film music, Milan ended up with quite a mixed spectrum of musical influence.

Boasske
Early 2019 Tom aka Joga Bonito, decided he wanted to start up a entirely new project with exotic beats and live music.Milan Beanz saw this opportunity to challenge himself and the world for a show that will musically take you through the amazon rainforest into the plains of the african savanna, all covered with live percussion, beatbox and live sampling. This is more than just a duo that will make your booties shake, it is an experience full of energy and good vibes that you will never forget. As challenging as it might seems, these guys are friends more than 16years and take you back to the roots of their brotherhood which you can feel on the dance floor so get ready for a true unique experience. And so Joga Bonito ( the show) was born and ready to blow your minds away.
